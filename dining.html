Meet Our Executive Chef

Michelin-Starred Culinary Artist

Chef Antoine Dubois brings over 20 years of international culinary experience to Dream Hotel. Trained in Paris and having worked in some of the world's most prestigious kitchens, Chef Dubois creates dishes that are both visually stunning and explosively flavorful. His philosophy centers on respecting ingredients and showcasing their natural qualities through innovative techniques.

"At Dream Hotel, we don't just serve meals - we create memorable dining experiences that engage all the senses. Every dish tells a story of passion, precision, and the pursuit of perfection."

— Chef Antoine Dubois
